Charge Sword

input: hold sword, then release

deals enough damage to instantly kill small and mid-sized enemies within a large area

has no cooldown but delays slightly

Slash Dash

input: press sword, then immediately press dash

a minor delay between inputs allows the sword attack to connect as well

great mobility tool that instantly kills small enemies, but has a cooldown

Chain Dash

input: press dash at the end of a previous dash

highest utility in the game, as platforming in certain areas is impossible without it

a chain of 5 or more builds momentum, causing you to slide once you drop the chain

Dash Strike

input: press sword during a dash

safe option for ending a chain dash if approaching enemies, though you will still slide afterward

heavily shoves enemies, causing them to collide with anything nearby; deal bonus damage to themselves and any other enemies struck
